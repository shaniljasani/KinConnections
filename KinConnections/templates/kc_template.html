About KC

As the pandemic has made it more challenging to physically connect with mentors, the creators of KIN-connections wanted to make sure that young people could still receive support and guidance. Connecting with mentors virtually provided the opportunity to connect people across borders. Notably, this program is mentee-driven, allowing for mentees to determine their goals, take initiative to connect to mentors who interest them, and guide the conversation(s) in a way that benefits them most. This helps young people gain important skills while receiving support and guidance through experienced mentors.
